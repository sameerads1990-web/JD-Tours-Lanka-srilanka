Our Story

Redefining Travel Since Inception

JD Tours Lanka was born out of a passion to provide unparalleled road transport experiences across the beautiful island of Sri Lanka. Based in Piliyandala, we have grown into one of the most trusted names in luxury transport.

Our philosophy is simple: delivering exceptional standard of service, prioritizing client safety, and ensuring every mile travelled feels like a first-class experience. Whether it's a corporate retreat, an extensive island tour, or a special private occasion, we bring elegance and reliability to your journey.
